Testimonials

BoltBus is a pretty solid method of transportation from NYC to Boston! I really liked the pickup spot, which is way better in my opinion than at Penn Station or at any other transit stop. Sometimes too overwhelming, especially when you are rushing to find your bus after being stuck in traffic. But I will be using Bolt Bus again! ---- Angel M. (Quincy, MA)

The bus driver was honestly the best. He was super friendly, answered everyone's questions and even took time to help an elderly lady who was seriously confused about where to get off at. The bus was clean and has plenty of leg room. The wifi works great, the seats are comfy, and there are outlets for your chargers. ---- Haylie L. (Washington, DC)

I am not a fan of flying, so I decided to start taking bus trips, and was VERY satisfied. The bus was on punctual, quiet, had plenty of leg room, a clean bathroom and a huge cargo area that allows folding bicycles. What more can you ask for?  I look forward to using the service again soon. Keep up the good work! ---- William S. (New York, NY)
